We have something to say. No shout. No sing from the top of our lungs! Or maybe just paint a picture. Or write a book. Or write an illustrated book. Or an animation, a film, or an interactive animated adventure. A immersive 3 Dimension environment? (Maybe next year) The point is: we have to make. We have to express, create, fabricate, tweak, modify, refactor, fix, rearrange, repurpose, reverse engineer, wreck, demolish, incinerate, and destroy. We have ideas! So many ideas and one way or another we have to get them out of our heads and into the real where they can live and breath and some day grow up to be a real boy.

Concophonic Parienoydoila::

An exploration of pariedolia within varying degrees of abstraction while confronting an extra-rational fear of non-representational drawing. The hardest thing in the world to draw is nothing. For me anyway. My hand fights tooth and nail to render every suggested tooth, imagined eyeball, and implied titty. Sometimes I succeed in this endeavour, sometimes I fail, and sometimes - more often than not honestly - I give up and wallow in the luxurious gluttony of all of those naughty little bits. -- Johrs Trulee
