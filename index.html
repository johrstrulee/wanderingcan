An Artful Anarchist Alinement

We have something to say. No shout. No sing from the top of our lungs! If doesn't work we can paint a picture. Or write a book. Or write an illustrated book. Or an animation, a film, or an interactive animated adventure. We have to make, tweak, modify,refactor, fix, rearrange, repurpose, reverse engineer, wreck, demolish, incinerate, and destroy. We do what we like and we do it with the like minded.

We ride the bleeding edge with bloodied hands and bleeding hearts.
